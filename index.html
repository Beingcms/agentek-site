The shift

From generic AI to your company’s intelligence.

The model is only the starting point. The advantage comes from teaching AI your language, your standards, your decision boundaries, and the context your people use every day.

Generic AI

Powerful, but disconnected from the business.

  • Does not understand internal language or decisions
  • Produces different answers across teams and tools
  • Cannot reliably follow company policies
  • Stays trapped in pilots and isolated demos
Company-specific AI

Grounded, teachable, and safe to put to work.

  • Uses your knowledge, terminology, and evidence
  • Learns from approved examples and human feedback
  • Operates within clear permissions and controls
  • Improves through measured real-world outcomes
How it works

Understand. Teach. Prove. Operate.

A clear path from general-purpose models to AI that works reliably inside the reality of your organisation.

01

Give AI your context

Connect company knowledge, meetings, terminology, structured data, policies, and permissions.

02

Teach it how you work

Adapt behaviour, tone, decisions, outputs, and tool usage using the right combination of prompting, retrieval, and model adaptation.

03

Prove it can be trusted

Test against real scenarios, edge cases, regulations, and business expectations before release.

04

Put it into operation

Deploy into real systems with human review, measurable outcomes, audit trails, and continuous improvement.
